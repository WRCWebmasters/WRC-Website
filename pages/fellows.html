Fellows

About

Each residential college has an Academic Fellows, an honorary service organization dedicated to supporting academic life in the college. Fellows are upperclassmen who were selected based on proven academic achievement and demonstrated willingness to help fellow students. As part of their responsibilities, Fellows provide free academic assistance on a regular basis through advertised review sessions, “office hours” in the college commons, and individual tutoring by request. Significant advantages of consulting a Fellow are the convenience and proximity of the source as well as the familiarity of the Fellow with the specific course.

Every Sunday, there is a Study Session from 7-9 pm. Please join us! Even if you have never been able to come to a session, this is a great time to start. We have tutors who can help in Science, Math, Engineering, English, FWIS, etc. The Fellows are here to help you with all of your academic needs.
