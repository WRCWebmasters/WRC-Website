President's Welcome: Jayaker Kolli


Hello New Phoenixes!

Welcome to Will Rice! I’m Jayaker, your college President. I, along with all other Will Ricers, can’t wait for y’all to experience Will Rice and all of its myth, power, and value.

Officially, my job is to represent you at the university level and run Will Rice’s student government, the Diet, which oversees the facilities of the college, budgets for events, and discusses college- and campus-wide issues. The Diet exists to enhance the experience of all Will Ricers, so if you have an idea or concern, don’t hesitate to bring it up!

That said, my most important role as President is to listen and serve as a resource for you – please call, text, email, message, or knock on my door (Old Dorm 110) with any questions (or just to say hi)!

I recognize that the transition into college can be stressful: there’s a lot of new people and ideas. During your time at Rice, you will be challenged, you will learn, and you will grow. But know that O-Week is built to guide you towards success. Your amazing O-Week coordinators have put an incredible amount of time and effort into crafting a week that introduces you to Rice and all of the support here for you.

While competition and tradition are certainly important parts of Will Rice culture, our tight-knit and inclusive community has defined my time here more than any other aspect. Will Ricers support each other, whether in a study group for Physics 101, at an acapella performance, or (of course) on the bike track.

Rice has a lot to offer, and that extends beyond the residential college system. Your first year is the best time to explore new ideas and seek new experiences. If an opportunity like a class or club interests you, even if you don’t know a whole lot about it, don’t be afraid to pursue it!

Always remember that for O-Week and beyond, you have the collective knowledge and support of hundreds of Will Ricers at your disposal. We’re so glad to have you here. Welcome to your new home!

Jayaker Kolli


Facilities

The first thing to know about Will Rice is that there are three separate buildings: The Commons, Old Dorm, and New Dorm. If the last two digits of your room number are 29 or less, you will be living in Old Dorm! If the last two digits of your room number are 30 or greater, you will be living in New Dorm!
