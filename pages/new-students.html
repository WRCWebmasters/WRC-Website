President's Welcome: Jayaker Kolli


Hello New Phoenixes!

Welcome to Will Rice! I’m Jayaker, your college President. While the other colleges are each great in their own way, Will Rice truly is the best. I, along with all other Will Ricers, can’t wait for y’all to experience Will Rice and all of its myth, power, and value.

My job as President is to represent you at the university level and run Will Rice’s student government (Diet). The Diet oversees the facilities of the college, budgeting for events and improvements, and discussing college- and campus-wide issues. The student government exists to enhance the experience of all Will Ricers, so if you have an idea or concern, don’t hesitate to bring it up! My most important role as President is to listen and serve as a resource for you – please call, text, email, message, or knock on my door (Old Dorm 110) if you ever need anything!

I recognize that the transition into college can be a stressful one: there’s a lot of new people and ideas. That said, know that O-Week is built to guide you through the transition and towards success. Your amazing O-Week coordinators have put an incredible amount of time and effort into crafting a week that introduces you to Rice and all of the people here to support you during your journey. During your time at Rice, you will be challenged, you will learn, and you will grow. O-Week is the first step, and your coordinators have made sure that this week will start you off on the right foot.

At first glance, Will Rice, with our intensely competitive spirit and rich history (like sweeping Beer Bike not once, not twice, but FIVE times), can be overwhelming. While competition and tradition are certainly important parts of Will Rice culture, our tight-knit community has defined my time here more than any other aspect. Will Ricers support each other, whether in a study group for Physics 101, at an a capella performance, or (of course) on the bike track. Regardless of how much time you spend in the Commons, the college will always be a community to fall back on and a base to jump off from.

Rice has a lot to offer, and that extends beyond the residential college system. Your first year is the best time to explore new ideas and seek new experiences. If an opportunity like a class or club interests you, even if you don’t know a whole lot about it, don’t be afraid to pursue it!

Always remember that for O-Week and beyond, you have the collective knowledge and support of hundreds of Will Ricers at your disposal. We’re so glad to have you here. Welcome to your new home!

Jayaker Kolli

Facilities

The first thing to know about Will Rice is that there are three separate buildings: The Commons, Old Dorm, and New Dorm. If the last two digits of your room number are 29 or less, you will be living in Old Dorm! If the last two digits of your room number are 30 or greater, you will be living in New Dorm!
