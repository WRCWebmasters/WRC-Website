Peer Career Advisors

About

Peer Career Advisors (PCAs) serve as ambassadors to undergraduates and play a valuable role in supporting the Center for Career Development (CCD). Through regular meetings and career cluster presentations, PCAs are well-informed to help students at Rice with career-related questions. PCAs reinforce the knowledge, skills, and resources offered through the CCD. PCAs are specially trained to assist with the following topics:

  • Resume and cover letter reviews
  • How to use Handshake
  • Internship & job search basics
  • Using CCD online resources (Focus2, Firsthand, etc.)
  • More services can be found on the CCD website

The Will Rice PCA Team is happy to help you if you would like to schedule a quick review or receive consultation or resource referrals. Please email us and we will answer at our soonest convenience.
